## Scoring Criteria

> Do not modify the criteria below. They are repo-agnostic and shared
> across all projects using this harness. See scoring-rubric.md for
> detailed score guides and penalty/reward lists.

### 1. Design Quality (HIGH WEIGHT — score /10)
Coherent whole with distinct identity. Museum quality is the target.
Generic = automatic fail.

### 2. Originality (HIGH WEIGHT — score /10)
Deliberate, custom creative choices. Penalize defaults and template patterns.

### 3. Craft (score /10)
Typography hierarchy, spacing rhythm, color discipline, contrast, responsive.

### 4. Functionality (score /10)
First-time visitor clarity: who, what, and what next — in under 5 seconds.

## Scoring Thresholds

| Criterion | Threshold | Action if below |
|---|---|---|
| Design Quality | 7/10 | Mandatory revision or full aesthetic pivot |
| Originality | 7/10 | Mandatory revision or full aesthetic pivot |
| Craft | 6/10 | Fix fundamentals before continuing |
| Functionality | 6/10 | Stop aesthetics work, fix usability first |
