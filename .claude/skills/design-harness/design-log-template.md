# Design Log Entry Template

Append an entry in this format to `design-log.md` after each iteration:

```markdown
## Iteration N — [YYYY-MM-DD]

**Strategy:** [What you changed and why. What aesthetic direction you are
  pursuing or pivoting to.]

**Design Quality:** X/10
**Originality:** X/10
**Craft:** X/10
**Functionality:** X/10

**Notes:** [What worked, what didn't, what a reviewer would likely flag.]

**Next move:** [Refine or pivot — and specifically why.]
```
